Intro

Document editing (64%) and data analytics (62%) are currently the most prevalent workplace uses for Generative AI. These figures are indicative of its general-purpose technology status.

Overview

This data shows the agility of Generative AI in performing repetitive and analytical tasks. Although the adoption rate is high in certain use cases, true transformational impact is still to be achieved across business functions.
